User Flow

Step-by-step journey through the app

1
Landing Page
Hero with World Cup 2026 branding. "Make your prediction" CTA. Brief explanation of the 2 images they'll get at the end.
2
Group Stage — 12 Groups
One group shown at a time. Each group has 4 teams. User picks 1st and 2nd place. Optional: enter predicted score for each of the 6 matches in the group.

3
Knockout Rounds
Auto-seeded from group picks. R32 → R16 → QF → SF → Final. Same card pattern: pick winner, optional score. Progress bar shows how far through the bracket they are.
4
Your Champion!
Champion reveal screen with confetti. Prompt to upload/take selfie to generate celebration image. Option to skip selfie and just get the bracket image.
5
Generate & Share
Loading screen while both images generate. Results page shows both images side by side with Download and Share buttons (saves to camera roll on mobile for WeChat/iMessage).
